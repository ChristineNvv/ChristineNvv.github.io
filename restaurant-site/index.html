From the famous Puerto Rican dish of Pernil Asado to lesser known treasures like our island coconut, mango or passion fruit sorbet, you'll find everything you crave on our menu. Whether you're searching for bowls, sandwiches, or mofongo, we've got you covered. When you visit our restaurant, you can't miss our must-try dishes, like our mouthwatering Puerto Rican chicken empanadas and beef empanadas.

And for something truly special, try our Smoked Chicken Bowl, Roasted Pork Bowl, or Boricua Bowl, all bursting with Caribbean flavor. Of course, we also offer plenty of traditional Puerto Rican favorites, like arroz con gandules y pernil, Mofongo with Bistec, Pallomilla, and many more. Make sure to add our delectable in-house sauces, which add an extra layer of flavor to any meal. And don't forget to try our mouthwatering shrimp dishes, which are sure to be a hit with seafood lovers!
